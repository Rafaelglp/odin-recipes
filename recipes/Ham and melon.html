Ham and melon

Description

Prosciutto and melon are paired to make a super easy, 2-ingredient, refreshing appetizer or snack: just cut the cantaloupe into bite-sized pieces, wrap them with ham and spear them on skewers!

Ingredients

  • 1 cantaloupe - seeded and cut into 8 wedges.
  • 8 thin slices prosciutto.

Steps

  1. Remove the flesh from the rind of the cantaloupe; wrap each piece of cantaloupe with a slice of prosciutto. Arrange on a platter and serve cold.
